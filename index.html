The Core Challenge

Every day, valuable resources are lost because recycling rules can be confusing. Unclear instructions lead to:

  • Confusing or inconsistent rules
  • Technical terminology that's hard to understand
  • Missed economic opportunities

Why It Matters

If recycling is ignored, we face increased landfill overflow, wasted natural resources, and higher pollution. Early awareness is key to reducing waste.
